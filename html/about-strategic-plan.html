Priority Areas

Six Strategic Pillars

The six priority areas that shape every WBF programme, partnership and investment between 2024 and 2028.

01

Regional Cooperation

Deepening cross-border cooperation between WBF member states through joint projects, shared institutions, and people-to-people exchange programmes.

  • 500+ cross-border projects
  • 6 regional networks strengthened
  • €15M in regional grants
02

Youth & Mobility

Expanding opportunities for young people to study, work, and volunteer across the Western Balkans, building a generation of regional citizens.

  • 10,000+ youth mobilities
  • 50+ university partnerships
  • Regional youth councils
03

Civil Society & Democracy

Supporting a vibrant civil society sector capable of holding governments accountable and driving democratic reforms across the region.

  • 300+ CSO grants
  • Regional CSO network
  • Advocacy capacity building
04

Environment & Climate

Advancing the green transition in the Western Balkans through environmental projects, climate education, and sustainable development initiatives.

  • €8M in green grants
  • 100+ environmental projects
  • Regional climate action plan
05

Digital & Innovation

Bridging the digital divide and fostering innovation ecosystems that connect the Western Balkans to European digital markets and standards.

  • Digital skills training
  • Tech startup support
  • E-governance cooperation
06

Institutional Effectiveness

Strengthening WBF's own systems, governance, and delivery capacity to ensure maximum impact, accountability and transparency.

  • ISO-certified processes
  • Digital grant management
  • Enhanced M&E framework
Roadmap

Implementation Timeline

A phased five-year plan moving from foundation to legacy, with a formal mid-term evaluation in 2026.

2024
Foundation
Launch new programmes & partnerships
Roll out the new strategic priorities, upgrade the OGMS platform, and onboard new bilateral donors.
2025
Scale
Double youth mobility, launch Partnership Platform
Expand Move Grants to twice the current call volume and launch the public WBF Partnership Platform.
2026
Deepen
Regional thematic networks & mid-term evaluation
Establish thematic networks under each pillar and conduct the independent mid-term evaluation of the plan.
2027
Expand
New donor partnerships & increased budget envelope
Secure additional multi-year donor commitments and expand the joint action portfolio with EU IPA III.
2028
Legacy
Final evaluation & next strategy development
Independent final evaluation, lessons-learned publication, and co-design of the 2029–2033 strategic framework with members and partners.
