Donors & Partners

Working alongside the region's key institutions

WBF cooperates with international donors and regional organisations that share its vision of a connected Western Balkans. Their political backing and financial commitment make our grants possible.

Strategic Donors

Multi-year cooperation agreements

The institutions providing the long-term funding and political backing that underpin every WBF programme. Together with the equal contributions of the six Contracting Parties, they make our work possible.

EU
European Union
Strategic donor · IPA III

Co-funds WBF through IPA III, the Instrument for Pre-accession Assistance. Joint action signed July 2021 and renewed through 2028.

Since 2021Co-fundingMulti-year
CH
Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation
Strategic donor · Bilateral

SDC joined as a strategic partner in June 2021, supporting WBF's regional cooperation agenda and capacity-building work.

Since 2021BilateralCapacity building
JP
Government of Japan
Strategic donor · Bilateral

Funds the LeadBalkans regional advocacy platform and the annual Advocacy & Networking Events programme.

LeadBalkansBilateralAdvocacy
DE
Federal Foreign Office of Germany
Strategic donor · Bilateral

Long-standing supporter of WBF's regional cooperation mission and EU integration agenda for the WB6.

BilateralEU integration
Bilateral & Multilateral Partners

Programmes co-designed with peer organisations

Funds and bilateral agencies that co-design and co-fund specific WBF programmes — most notably the Western Balkans–Visegrad Fellowship and the Italian Agency cooperation framework.

V4
International Visegrad Fund
Programme partner

Joint operator of the Western Balkans–Visegrad Fellowship — supporting WB6 doctoral students and graduates in V4 host institutions.

Since 2017Visegrad FellowshipMobility
IT
Italian Agency for Development Cooperation (AICS)
Bilateral partner

Cooperation agreement signed 2024–2025 supporting WBF's regional cooperation portfolio and joint capacity-building activities.

Since 2024Bilateral
Foundations

Philanthropic partners
