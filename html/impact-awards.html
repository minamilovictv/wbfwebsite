WBF Champion Awards

Recognising outstanding contributions to regional cooperation, reconciliation and European integration across the Western Balkans — celebrating the people, organisations and partnerships behind the change.

About the Awards

Four categories, six countries, one region

The WBF Champion Awards run annually. A jury of regional experts and former laureates selects winners from across the WB6 in four categories — recognising both individual and institutional contributions.

Champion Project
The funded project that most exceeded expectations in scope, partnership quality and lasting regional impact during the past call cycle.
Champion Organisation
A grantee organisation that has demonstrated sustained excellence across multiple calls, in delivery, transparency and regional impact.
Champion Partnership
A consortium whose collaboration set the standard for cross-border cooperation, with all partners punching above their weight.
Lifetime Contribution
An individual whose career has shaped civil society, reconciliation or regional cooperation across the Western Balkans over many years.
Young Leader
A leader under 35 whose work as a grantee or fellow has had outstanding regional impact, recognising the next generation of changemakers.
Special Jury Prize
A discretionary award given when the jury wishes to honour a contribution that doesn't fit the other categories but deserves recognition.
