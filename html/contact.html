Contact the Western Balkans Fund

The WBF Secretariat is based in Tirana, Albania. Send us a message, drop in, or reach the right team directly for your enquiry — we respond within five working days.

General inquiries
Anything you can't find elsewhere — partnerships, press, general questions about WBF.
info@westernbalkansfund.org
Programme questions
Specific to a programme call, eligibility, partnerships or evaluation queries.
grants@westernbalkansfund.org
OGMS technical support
Account issues, upload errors and submission troubleshooting on the OGMS platform.
ogms@westernbalkansfund.org
Press & media
Journalists, photographers and broadcasters — interview requests and media accreditation.
press@westernbalkansfund.org

Send us a message

Use the form below for general inquiries. For OGMS technical issues, use the direct email above — that team monitors a separate queue and responds faster.
