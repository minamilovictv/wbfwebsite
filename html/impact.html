Geographic Reach

Six Contracting Parties, one region

Every WBF project must cross at least three of the six Western Balkans territories. Projects covering all six are given priority in evaluation. The numbers below reflect the cumulative project participation rate per country across all WBF calls.

🇦🇱Albania82 projects
🇧🇦Bosnia and Herzegovina76 projects
🇽🇰Kosovo*68 projects
🇲🇰North Macedonia71 projects
🇲🇪Montenegro74 projects
🇷🇸Serbia79 projects

*Without prejudice to positions on status, in line with UNSCR 1244/1999 and the ICJ Opinion on the Kosovo declaration of independence.
