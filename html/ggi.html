About the Programme

Grassroots cooperation, at a regional scale

The Good Governance and Integration (GGI) grants scheme is the Western Balkans Fund's flagship programme. Since the first call in 2017, GGI has funded hundreds of cross-border cooperation projects led by civil society, cultural institutions, universities and grassroots organisations across the Western Balkans.

Each project must bring together partners from at least three Contracting Parties — a deliberate design choice that turns funding into a tool for region-building, not just bilateral exchange. Projects span culture, civic engagement, education, youth, environmental protection and democratic governance.

The scheme is co-funded by the European Union under IPA III and by the six Contracting Parties through their equal contributions, providing a stable, predictable funding stream that civil society organisations across the region can count on year after year.

The eighth call (Call No. 8) closed in November 2025 and received a record 263 applications. Evaluation is now underway, with results expected in June 2026 and project implementation starting July 2026.

Intervention Areas

What GGI funds

GGI supports cross-border projects in six broad areas. Applications are welcome at the intersection of multiple areas — many of the strongest projects combine, say, culture with civic engagement, or education with environmental protection.

Good Governance & Anti-Corruption
Citizen oversight of public institutions, transparency initiatives, integrity in public procurement, regional anti-corruption monitoring.
Civil Society Cooperation
Strengthening CSO networks across borders, joint advocacy initiatives, capacity exchange, and coalition-building on regional issues.
EU Integration
Citizen engagement with EU integration, monitoring of accession reforms, regional information campaigns, alignment with EU acquis.
Cultural Heritage & Dialogue
Shared cultural heritage, intercultural dialogue, museums and arts cooperation, oral history projects across borders.
Education & Youth Engagement
University and school cooperation, regional youth networks, civic education, exchange programmes, lifelong learning.
Environment & Climate
Cross-border environmental protection, climate awareness, sustainable development, green civil society initiatives.
